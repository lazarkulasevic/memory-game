ENTER YOUR NAME AND PICK A LEVEL




Memory Game built from scratch using only HTML5, CSS3 & vanilla Javascript.

When the game starts, all cards are turned face down. The player then flips over two cards, selecting them by clicking on them. If the two cards have the same image, they remain face up. If not, they are flipped face down again after a short delay. When all cards on the board are facing up the timer stops and the results board pops up with top five player's names.
